# VGChartz-2024-Video-Game-Sales-Analytics
This project analyzes the VGChartz 2024 video game sales dataset — comprising 64,016 game records across 81 platforms, 20 genres, and hundreds of publishers — to uncover business-relevant insights about the global gaming industry.

## 📊 Key Insights (SMART + Analytics Approach)

---

### 🔍 1. Descriptive Analytics — What Happened?

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Global Sales | 6,605.91M units |
| North America Sales Share | 50.6% (3,345.52M units) |
| Top Genre (Sports) | 1,187.51M units |
| Top Genre (Action) | 1,125.89M units |
| Best-Selling Title | Grand Theft Auto V – PS3 (20.32M units) |
| Top Console | PS2 (1,027.76M units) |
| Peak Sales Era | 2006–2012 (PS3 & Xbox 360 Generation) |

> North America dominates global video game sales, with Action and Sports as the
> leading genres. The PS2 remains the highest-selling console in the dataset.

---

### 🔎 2. Diagnostic Analytics — Why Did It Happen?

- 🌎 **North America's 50.6% share** is driven by high console adoption rates,
  a large installed base, and stronger consumer purchasing power
- 🇯🇵 **Japan's lower 10.4% contribution** reflects a cultural preference for
  handheld platforms (DS, PSP) and RPGs over Western AAA franchises
- 📈 **The 2006–2012 peak** coincides with the rise of annual franchises like
  Call of Duty, which consistently drove Shooter genre dominance
- ⭐ **Titles with critic scores above 8.5** consistently outperform
  lower-scored titles — review scores materially influence purchase decisions

---

### 📈 3. Predictive Analytics — What Will Happen?

- 🎮 Titles scoring **above 8.0** on PS5/Xbox Series X are projected to
  surpass **5M units within 12 months** of launch
- 🔄 The **Nintendo Switch** is on track to surpass PS4 total units
  given its growing sales momentum
- 🗺️ **RPG and Action-Adventure genres** show a rising trend since 2015
  and are projected to dominate the 2020s era
- 🏢 Publisher consolidation (**EA, Activision, Ubisoft** — 700M+ units each)
  suggests the gap between major and indie publishers will widen further

---

### 💡 4. Prescriptive Analytics — What Should Be Done?

- 🚀 **Prioritize North America + PAL markets** for AAA launches —
  combined they represent over 80% of global sales
- 🕹️ **Japan-focused publishers** should invest in handheld/hybrid
  platforms rather than home console exclusives
- 📝 **Early critic review outreach** (advance copies, preview builds)
  can measurably improve launch-week sales given the score-to-sales correlation
- 💻 **Indie developers** should target niche genres on PC
  (12,617+ titles hosted) as a low-cost, high-reach entry point
- 🔁 **Cross-platform releases** (e.g., GTA V across 4 consoles)
  significantly multiply total units sold
